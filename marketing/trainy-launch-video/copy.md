# Trainy launch film copy

No narration is used. These are the only authored editorial lines in the main
film; every other readable product label comes from captured Trainy UI.

## Final film copy

1. `Going somewhere?`
2. `Meet your train.`
3. `Tokyo → Shin‑Osaka`
4. `Nozomi 231 · 09:21`
5. `Utrecht Centraal`
6. `What leaves next?`
7. `Live when it’s live.`
8. `Clear when it isn’t.`
9. `Built with Codex.`
10. `Verified in Simulator.`
11. `Know before you go.`

The required post-film credit is `Created with GPT‑5.6 Sol + Skills`.

## Seven-sweep copy review

| Sweep | Result |
| --- | --- |
| Clarity | Each line is understandable without narration or product knowledge. |
| Voice and tone | Conversational, calm, and traveler-first; no corporate or technical voice remains. |
| So what | The arc answers a traveler’s real question: what is my train doing, and can I trust this state? |
| Prove it | Route, time, departures, live/unavailable state, retry, privacy, and Simulator verification are shown in real captured UI. |
| Specificity | Tokyo, Shin‑Osaka, Utrecht Centraal, Nozomi 231, 09:21, Codex, and Simulator replace vague claims. |
| Heightened emotion | Curiosity becomes recognition, confidence, and a concise promise. |
| Zero risk | No App Store, user-count, worldwide-live, realtime-everywhere, or performance claim is made. |

## Editorial panel

The final lines were re-scored after removing architecture, audit, Build Week,
and capability-classification copy from the main story.

| Editorial persona | Score | Final assessment |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Launch copywriter | 9/10 | Strong hook-to-promise arc; the Build Week fact stays inside the product journey. |
| UX writer | 9/10 | Short, scannable lines leave the product enough visual space. |
| Traveler / skeptical customer | 8/10 | Route and availability language are concrete and trustworthy. |
| Brand strategist | 9/10 | Distinctly Trainy: quiet confidence, source truth shown through product behavior, and no judge-facing jargon. |

Average: **8.75/10**. Every score is at least 7 and the average is above 8.

## Rejected claims

The film does not say that all providers are live, starter catalog data is
realtime, Shinkansen includes live vehicle positions, the app is on the App
Store, the product has users, or an unmeasured performance target was met.
